## Tips

1. **Photos are everything** — listings with real photos get 3-5x more clicks. Take well-lit photos of each room and shared spaces.
2. **Repost frequently** — FB Marketplace: every 3-5 days. Craigslist: renew every 48 hours.
3. **Post timing** — Best times: Sunday evening, Monday-Wednesday morning. People search for rooms early in the week.
4. **Always link to fruitfulrooms.com** — Drive traffic through your screening form instead of just getting texts/calls.
5. **Use city-specific Facebook groups** — Search "[City] rooms for rent", "[City] housing", "[City] roommates" and join 5-10 groups per market.
6. **Furnished Finder** — Mention proximity to hospitals for traveling nurse traffic.
